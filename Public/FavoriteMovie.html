My Favorite Video Games

This is a list of some of my favorite video games.

1. Baldur's Gate 3

This was one of my favorite games I have ever played. The developers thought of every little detail and it really shows.

2. World of Warcraft

I started playing this back in 2008 and I still play it to this day. I have played every expansion and I just love how fun the gameplay loop is and how much joy it still brings me to play it today.

3. Halo 3

Nostalgia trip! This game took up a lot of my childhood and I still have some very fond memories of it. I still jump back in and play it every now and then just for fun.
